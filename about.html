Who Are We

We are a program management non-governmental organization that designs and implements data-driven, evidence-based interventions to create sustainable social change.

Our work focuses on improving outcomes in education, gender equity, physical health and wellbeing, mental health, and poverty and hunger among underserved and marginalized communities.

See Our Programs

What We Do

We implement programs that create positive change in underserved communities. Our focus areas include:

  • Education: Increasing access to quality education for children and youths.
  • Gender Equity: Empowering women and girls to achieve equal opportunities.
  • Health & Wellbeing: Promoting physical health, mental health awareness, and access to healthcare services.
  • Poverty & Hunger: Supporting initiatives that improve livelihoods and reduce hunger.

Through these programs, we help young people access opportunities, resources, and support to reach their full potential.

Our Strategic Approach

A systematic, evidence-based methodology that ensures maximum impact and sustainable outcomes.

1

Develop

We develop data-driven and evidence-based sustainable intervention programs through comprehensive research and analysis of community needs.

2

Implement

We implement these programs to target and stimulate positive outcomes and impacts in underserved communities across Nigeria.

3

Educate

We aim to educate individuals and communities through our interventions, building capacity and knowledge for long-term sustainability.

4

Empower

Our interventions are designed to empower marginalized groups and promote gender equity, creating lasting change from within communities.

5

Evaluate

We track, monitor, and evaluate the effectiveness and cost-efficiency of our interventions to ensure continuous improvement and accountability.
